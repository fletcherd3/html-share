do a focused online-assessment prep sprint

the immediate, low-risk move is a short, distraction-free preparation block: refresh problem-solving mechanics, rehearse communicating your reasoning, and verify your setup. this page deliberately contains no personal application, reminder, or calendar information.

recommended prep sequence

  1. simulate one timed practice: choose a representative coding/problem-solving exercise and work under a fixed timer.
  2. use a spoken-reasoning pass: state assumptions, edge cases, complexity, and validation checks as you go.
  3. finish with setup hygiene: stable connection, charger, quiet workspace, permitted tools/policies, and a backup plan.
  4. review only the gaps: write down one or two weak patterns, then practise those—not an unfocused marathon.
